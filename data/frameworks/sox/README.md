## Versions to Cover
Focus on PCAOB auditing standards most relevant to internal control over financial reporting (ICFR):

1. SOX Act 2002 — Original legislation (Sections 302, 404, 409)
2. PCAOB AS 2 (2004) → PCAOB AS 5 (2007): Risk-based approach, scalable for company size
3. PCAOB AS 5 → AS 2201 (2017): Codification (renumbering only, no substantive change)
4. PCAOB QC 1000 (2024): New quality control standard — significant change for CPA firms

## PCAOB QC 1000 (2024) — Major Change
QC 1000 is the most significant PCAOB development since AS 5. It overhauled quality control requirements for CPA firms performing PCAOB-registered audits:
- Required for all firms with fiscal years beginning January 1, 2025+
- New governance and leadership requirements
- Enhanced independence monitoring
- Technology and innovation acknowledgment
- Engagement quality reviews strengthened
